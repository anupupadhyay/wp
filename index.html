New Renovations to Cinema

We have revamped the movie viewing experience at Silverado completely. Now, we have two cinemas, Maxima and Rivola. Maxima is our largest Cinema with with over 50 seats available with 13 beanbags that seat 2 adults or 3 children. Maxima plays only the latest and greatest films, with a massive 1000 inch screen! We also have 12 gold class seats for ultimate viewing pleasure, the perfect size for total immersion.

Our second cinema, Rivola, plays only the classiest and critically acclaimed arts and foreign films, this venue can be hired out for both public and private events, catering included. The interior is a lovingly created art deco original.

Visit us now for an astounding movie experience!
